About us

Bladez & Fadez Barbershop was created for the modern man. We're a locally owned, and operated family business.

Most hair salons are designed for women, leaving guys feeling like they walked into the wrong place to get their haircut.
Bladez & Fadez Barbershop was created as a place for men or boys so they too, would have a place where they feel most comfortable while getting a haircut.

We brought back those old fashion barber shop services all guys enjoy; straight razor shaves, relaxing scalp massage & shampoo with a hot towel treatment. We also offer several other services guys such as beard trimming and shaving.

We're always current with men's hairstyle trends as well as boys hairstyle trends so you don't have to worry.

Bladez & Fadez Barbershop specializes in Men's Haircuts, Boys Haircuts, and many other barbershop services.
We cut men's hair all day every day, and we're really good at it.
We know what looks good on you, your son, your father, and your grandfather too. We know the trends in men's hair. We'll make sure all the males in your family look amazing!
